# Code of Conduct

STOOP follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

**Summary:** be respectful, constructive, and welcoming. Project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) are for collaboration, not conflict.

**Enforcement:** report concerns to [kjricciardi@gmail.com](mailto:kjricciardi@gmail.com). Reports are reviewed by the maintainer and handled with discretion.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
